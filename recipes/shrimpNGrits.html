Shrimp N Grits

Low Country Shrimp 'n' Grits

This hometown treasure is filled with history and flavor. This dish is made up of fresh shrimp, creamy grits, and sauteed onions and tomatoes, sourrounded by savory southern gravy.


Ingredients

    Grits

  • Boiling water(or milk)-2 cup
  • Stone ground grits-1 cup
  • Sugar-2 tbsp
  • Salt-2 tbsp
  • Butter-4tbsp
  • Cheese(optional)-1/2 cup

    • Shrimp Sauce

  • Shrimp(tail on)-6 to 8 pieces
  • Cherry tomatoe halves-1 cup
  • Red Onion(Julliane)-1 large
  • Amber Beer-4oz
  • Beef stock(reduced/thickened)-1 cup
  • Scallions(thin sliced) for garnish

Steps

    Grits

  1. Add grits to boiling water or milk. Stir until Thick.
  2. add sugar, and salt.
  3. Stir in butter, and Cheese(optional).
  4. Set aside.

    Shrimp Sauce

  1. Sear shrimp sin a saute pan, flip after one side gets color.
  2. Add onions and tomatoes, cook until soft
  3. Deglaze pan with amber beer
  4. Add reduced stock and season
  5. Pour over grits, and garnish with scallions
