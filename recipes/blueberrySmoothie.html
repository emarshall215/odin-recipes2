Blueberry Smoothies

Fresh low calorie Blueberry Smoothie

This healthy option can serve as a great post workout energy boost, or healthy late night snack. This amzing smoothie is full of Antioxidants and flavor.


Ingredients

  • Frozen Blueberries-6oz
  • Oat milk-2cups
  • Frozen bannana-1/2
  • Greek Yogurt-2oz
  • Brown Sugar-1 tbsp

Steps

  1. Blend blueberries, bannanas, yogurt, and milk
  2. Add brown sugar if smoothie needs more sweetness
  3. Pour smoothie in a cold glass, and garnish with fresh blueberries, and mint(optional)
