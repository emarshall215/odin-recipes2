Neopolitan Style Pizza

An authentic handmade pizza, made in the style of Naples. This recipe will show you both how to make the dough as well as the topppings.


Ingredients

    Bread Dough

  • (00)Flour-500g
  • Water-1 1/2 cup
  • Yeast-4g
  • Salt-25g

    • Toppings

  • Crushed(San Marzano) Tomatoes-3oz
  • diced/shredded Mozzerella-2oz
  • Sliced Pepperoni-3oz
  • Olive Oil-2oz

Steps

    Bread Dough

  1. Dissolve salt in water.
  2. Mix in 1/4 of flour, then add the yeast.
  3. Add rest of flour gradually while mixing until dough ball forms.
  4. kneed dough until smooth, cover, and let rise for 1 hour.
  5. cut dough into 280g pieces, roll into balls, and cover. Let rest overnight.
  6. when ready carefully stretch dough (dont flatten the edges) and place on peel.

    Toppings

  1. Spread crushed tomatoes on your fresh made pizza dough.
  2. sprinkle mozzarella on top of dough evenly.
  3. lay out pepperonis evenly as well.
  4. Drizzle olive oil around the edge of the pizza, and on the toppings
  5. Cook in 500 degree oven, on a pizza stone until golden brown
  6. Slice, serve, and enjoy
