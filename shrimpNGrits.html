Low Country Shrimp 'N' Grits

"Charleston Style" Shrimp n Grits"

This hometown favorite is packed full of both flavor and history. Creamy stone groung grits, and buttery gulf shrimp with amber beer gravy


Ingredients

  • Stone Ground grits- 1 Cup
  • Boiling water or milk-2cups
  • sugar- 2 tbsp
  • Salt- 2 tbsp
  • cold butter-4-8oz
  • Gulf Shrimp-10-12
  • Cherry Tomatos (cut in half)- 1 cup
  • Sliced Red Onion- 1/2 (med) red onion
  • Demi glas (or beef stock)- 2 Cup
  • Amber beer- 1/2 cup

Steps

  1. stir grits into boilin water or milk. Add sugar and salt to season. stir until thick, Finish with cold butter.
  2. Sear shrimp, add tomatos, and onions, cook until soft.
  3. Deglaze the pan with amber beer, and add demi, or beef stock
  4. when sauce is thick, plate creamy grits, and pour shrimp over the top with the sauce.
